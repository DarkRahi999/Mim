Flowers

Flowers are the reproductive structures of flowering plants that are essential for reproduction through pollination, which allows them to produce seeds for new plants. They are diverse in color, shape, and scent, and play a vital role in ecosystems by providing food for insects and birds, and have numerous uses for humans, including decoration, food, and medicine.
